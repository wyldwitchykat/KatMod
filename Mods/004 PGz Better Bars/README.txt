PGz modlets are created and written by Phys1csGamez.

"004 PGz Better Bars" is a modlet that implements three way corner bars to make complete cages and removes collider protection from bars.
This modlet will show the variant shapes in the PGz varient shape menu.
Modlet "000 PGz Variant Blocks" must be install for this modlet to work.

Videos to support the installation of PGz modlets can be found at https://www.youtube.com/@Phys1csGamez
