PGz modlets are created and written by Phys1csGamez.
https://www.youtube.com/@Phys1csGamez

"005 PGz Ramp 6m Magic Blocks" is a modlet that provides corner and filler blocks for all "ramp 6m" existing shapes.
Inspired by a video by JaWoodle [https://www.youtube.com/@JaWoodle] where he utilised these blocks to play with the pathing of the zombies.

These blocks allow the completion of the "zombie wall" and the production of new and interesting buildings and bases. Let your imagination go wild.

This modlet will show the variant shapes in the PGz varient shape menu.
Modlet "000 PGz Variant Blocks" must be installed for this modlet to work.

Videos to support the installation of PGz modlets can be found at https://www.youtube.com/@Phys1csGamez
